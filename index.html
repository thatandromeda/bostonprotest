These are all the people who tweeted a clear opinion on the #bostonprotest hashtag on 15 January 2015, from the time that I-93 was shut down until 12:27pm.

I made this page to make it more apparent that reactions to the protest are not racially neutral. My black friends and colleagues have experiences in this country that I do not, and the fact that I don't always see does not absolve me of the responsibility to look.
